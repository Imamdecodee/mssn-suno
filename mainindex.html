Our Mission

Mission Statement

To establish an Islamic society where members can practice Islam in its entirety, promote unity among Muslims, and contribute positively to the development of Summit University Offa and the society at large through educational, religious, and social activities.

Our Vision

Vision Statement

To be a leading MSSN branch that produces morally upright, academically sound, and socially responsible Muslim students who will serve as agents of positive change in the university community and beyond.
